## Summary

- **Total Notifications**: 240 (8 per day Г— 30 days)
- **Notification Types**: 2 (Sehri & Iftar)
- **Reminders per Prayer**: 4 (15min, 10min, 5min, exact time)
- **Duas Included**: Yes (at exact prayer times)
- **Persistence**: Yes (survives app closure)
- **Boot Persistence**: Yes (with RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED)
